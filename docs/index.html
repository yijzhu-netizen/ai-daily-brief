Two Configurations: 325 Miles and 500 Miles Range

The Tesla Semi launches in two variants. The Standard Range delivers 325 miles (523 km) on a full charge, targeting regional trucking routes. The Long Range version achieves up to 500 miles (805 km), making it viable for cross-state hauling. Both variants use an 822 kWh battery pack — among the largest ever deployed in a production vehicle.

Efficiency testing at a Gross Combined Weight of 82,000 lbs (the maximum legal Class 8 load) recorded 1.68 kWh per mile — a 10% improvement over earlier prototypes. Fast charging delivers a full recharge in approximately 30 minutes via Tesla's new Megacharger network.

🔋 Battery: 822 kWh — among the largest in any production vehicle
